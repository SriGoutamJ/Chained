Chained Co.




Chained Co. is a Supply Chain Management System.

We worked together to design, create and produce the one tool that any supply chain needs, An efficient management system.

We all know that the ultimate goal of any supply chain is to fulfil the clients' requested products all the way from the place that they have been brought into existence. We've created one tool that is required to ease the journey of fulfling the clients' request, right from procuring the product from the best dealer, inventorising it, processing the clients' request, generating invoices, handling logistics and the various other processes involved, all while remaining effective and efficient.

With this 'one stop for all' tool that we've created, we hope to assist you bringing smiles in the faces of all the parties so involved.
